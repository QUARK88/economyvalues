EconomyValues

What is EconomyValues ?

EconomyValues is one of the 4 tests of the AllValues series. Its goal is to class economic beliefs in a precise and simple manner. Base idea by u/rayz9989.

What are the 14 values ?

There are 7 independent axes - Regulation, Property, Repartition, Trade, Work, Control and Industry - and each has two opposing values assigned to them :

Regulation ( Regulation )
Those with higher Regulation scores believe that economic practices should face reglementations in order to avoid possible dangers. Those with high scores might support planning.

Laissez Faire ( Regulation )
Those with higher Laissez Faire scores belive that the market should be trusted to organize the economy correctly. Those with high scores might support economic chaos.

State ( Property )
Those with higher State scores belive that there are sectors that should be nationalized to ensure a better use of resources. Those with high scores might support socialism.

Private ( Property )
Those with higher Private scores belive that industry is better when posessed by private groups. Those with high scores might support privatism.

Equality ( Repartition )
Those with higher Equality scores belive that economic inequalities should be diminished. Those with high scores might support communism.

Wealth ( Repartition )
Those with higher Wealth scores belive that inequality is justified to promote hard working individuals first. Those with high scores might support corporate welfare.

Protectionism ( Trade )
Those with higher Protectionism scores belive that relying on foreign resources is bad and endagers local economies. Those with high scores might support autarky.

Free Trade ( Trade )
Those with higher Free Trade scores belive that global trade with other nations is needed to thrive economically. Those with high scores might support interdependence.

Workers ( Work )
Those with higher Workers scores belive that the workplace should be organized horizontally. Those with high scores might support worker ownership.

Owners ( Work )
Those with higher Owners scores belive that the owners deserve to rule over their employees. Those with high scores might support brutality.

Monopoly ( Control )
Those with higher Monopoly scores belive that the economy is better when united in big groups. Those with high scores might support monopolies.

Competition ( Control )
Those with higher Competition scores belive that local competition between small compagnies is the way to go. Those with high scores might support duellism.

Environment ( Industry )
Those with higher Environment scores belive that the protection of the environment is a priority. Those with high scores might support deep ecology.

Industry ( Industry )
Those with higher Industry scores belive that productivity should be promoted first at the cost of some nature. Those with high scores might support anthropocentrism.

What is the " Closest Match " thing at the bottom of the results ?

The Closest Match is a feature in progress that will select the ideology that fits the most your views. If you find it innacurate, send your results with your ideology name to the adresses below.
